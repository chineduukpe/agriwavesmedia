About us

About Agriwaves TV

Agriwaves media value

We are a development communication company for influencing growth and transforming agriculture and nutrition in Africa. We recognize how essential communication is in the development of sustainable food systems. Our strategic solutions and innovations are evidence-driven, people-oriented and results-based. We build partnerships with private sector organisations, governments, NGOs, and anyone who interested in the promotion of food security. We neutralize barriers and open new vistas for sustainable development through design and implementation of quality strategic communications.

Our Mission

  • To engage in the design and implementation of technical communication innovatively through evidence-based strategies.
  • To utilize communication as a tool for enhancing development of food systems across Africa.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be leading agricultural media organisation in Africa.
